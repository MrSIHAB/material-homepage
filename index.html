Key Features

🎨

Modern Design

Clean and visually appealing interface inspired by Material Design principles.

🛡️

Privacy First

100% privacy proof. No personal data is collected, and all preferences are stored locally.

🔍

Multiple Search Engines

Support for various search engines including Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and more.

🌓

Dynamic Themes

Automatic light and dark mode with customizable color palettes.

🔗

Quick Shortcuts

User-defined shortcut sites for easy access to your most-used web applications.

💾

Backup & Restore

Easily backup and restore your settings locally to keep your dashboard consistent.
